What We Farm

Livestock

We believe in free range farming principles taught by Joe Salatin. Our Cows, chickens, and pigs are grown in a way that doesn't harm them, or nature. Animals belong in nature and not in a cage, and we pride ourselves in having our animals in nature

Produce

Organic produce through permaculture practices. Through these practices, we produce sweeter, and more nutrient dense crops that help sustain the environment, and help sustain families.From fruit, to fresh vegetables from season to season, you name it. No chemicals around these parts!

Honey

On site, we have bees which help pollinate all of our crops throughout the land. They not only benefit us through pollination, but we benefit from their natural sugar source: Honey.
